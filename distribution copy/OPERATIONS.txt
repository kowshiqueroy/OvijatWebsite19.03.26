# Food Distribution System - Operational Guide

This document provides a step-by-step guide for each user role in the system.

---

## 1. ADMIN (Full Access)
**Responsibility:** System setup, user management, and master cleanup.
- **Cleanup:** You have a "Trash" icon in Sales, Customers, and Transactions. Use this to remove test data or mistakes. It handles related records (like items or load assignments) automatically.
- **Setup:** Configure company details in "Company Settings" for correct headers on invoices.
- **Monitoring:** Use the "Dashboard" and "Comprehensive Reports" for a birds-eye view of the business.

---

## 2. ACCOUNTANT (Finance & Approvals)
**Responsibility:** Financial integrity and confirming orders.
- **Approving Sales:** 
  1. Go to "Pending Confirmations".
  2. Check the "Capability" color. Red means they owe more than their credit limit.
  3. Click "Confirm" to deduct stock and charge the customer wallet.
- **Managing Money:** 
  1. Go to "Customer Ledger" for a detailed statement.
  2. To hide a mistake from the printed statement, toggle "Hidden from Print" on the transaction or invoice.
- **Manual Adjustments:** 
  1. View a customer.
  2. Use "(+) Add Balance" for payments (Received cash/bank).
  3. Use "(-) Deduct Balance" for charges.
  4. **CRITICAL:** Always enter bank details and who authorized the payment in the description.

---

## 3. MANAGER (Operations & Warehouse)
**Responsibility:** Stock control and delivery execution.
- **Stock In:** Use "Inventory > Stock In" to add new arrivals.
- **Stock Damages:** Use "Inventory > Stock Damages" to record expired or broken goods immediately.
- **Logistics (The Truck Load Workflow):**
  1. Go to "Logistics > Truck Loads".
  2. Click "New Load from Sales".
  3. Select multiple "Confirmed" invoices.
  4. Enter Truck No, Driver Name, Source, and Destination.
  5. Once the truck leaves, update the load status to "Departed".
  6. When the driver returns, update the individual status of each invoice (Delivered/Failed/Returned).

---

## 4. SR / SALES REPRESENTATIVE (Sales Entry)
**Responsibility:** Order taking in the field.
- **New Order:** Use "POS / New Sale" to create a "Draft".
- **Tracking:** You can only see your own sales in "My Sales". Check "Delivery Status" to update your customers on their shipment.

---

## 5. CUSTOMER (Self-Service)
**Responsibility:** Placing orders and viewing history.
- **Ordering:** Use the POS to create a draft order.
- **Statement:** View your current wallet balance and previous invoices in your portal.
- **Verification:** You can scan the QR code on any printed invoice to verify it online.

---

## 🏗️ SYSTEM MAINTENANCE NOTES
- **Soft Delete:** The system uses `isDelete = 0` logic. "Deleting" a record hides it from the UI but keeps it in the database for auditing.
- **Wallet Model:** 
  - (+) Balance means customer has credit (Prepaid).
  - (-) Balance means customer owes money (Debt).
- **Stock:** Stock can go negative to allow for fast operations; ensure "Stock In" is performed as soon as goods arrive to correct this.
